General Dentistry

Your foundation for a lifetime of healthy smiles.

We believe that great oral health starts with consistent, preventive care. Our General Dentistry services are designed to keep your teeth and gums healthy.

Dental Check-up & Consultation

A thorough examination of your teeth, gums, and overall oral health. During your visit, our dentist will assess your dental condition, discuss any concerns you may have, and create a personalised care plan tailored to your needs. We recommend check-ups every six months to stay ahead of potential issues.

Scaling & Polishing

Removes plaque and tartar for a clean smile.

Dental X-rays

Even with regular brushing and flossing, plaque and tartar can build up in hard-to-reach areas. Our professional scaling and polishing treatment removes these stubborn deposits, leaving your teeth feeling noticeably cleaner, smoother, and brighter. It's one of the simplest yet most effective ways to protect your long-term oral health.

Gum Disease Treatment

Healthy gums are the foundation of a healthy mouth. If you're experiencing bleeding, swelling, or sensitivity in your gums, you may be showing early signs of gum disease. We offer targeted treatments to halt the progression of gum disease, restore gum health, and prevent tooth loss — so you can keep your natural teeth for longer

Bad Breath Management

Persistent bad breath (halitosis) is often a sign of an underlying dental or health concern rather than just a hygiene issue. Our team will identify the root cause — whether it's gum disease, bacteria buildup, dry mouth, or other factors — and provide effective, lasting solutions so you can speak and smile with confidence.
